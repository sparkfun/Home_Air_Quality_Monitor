


The firmware will upload 11 datapoints to the application. They are stored in the following order:

0: CO2 PPM - PASCO2
1: PPM 1.0 - SEN 
2: PPM 2.5 - SEN
3: PPM 4.0 - SEN
4: PPM 10.0 - SEN
5: Humidity - SEN
6: Temperature - SEN
7: VOCs - SEN
8: CO - MQ7
9: NG - MQ4
10: AQI - Computed onboard using PPM 2.5 and other values
